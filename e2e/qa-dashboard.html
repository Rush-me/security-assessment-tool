The E2E framework enforces clean division of concerns to isolate changes:

  • Feature Files: Written in Gherkin business syntax to allow review by Product Owners.
  • Step Definitions: Act as glue logic, mapping regular expressions in feature steps to Page actions.
  • Page Objects: Isolate DOM CSS/XPath locators and element interactions. All selectors (e.g. mat-select[formcontrolname="confidentiality"]) are restricted here.
